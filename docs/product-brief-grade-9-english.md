# Product Brief: Grade 9 English

## Positioning

VietEd helps Grade 9 students in Vietnam improve English faster through AI-guided mistake repair.

Core promise:

"VietEd helps students improve faster by identifying the exact English mistakes holding them back and generating the right next practice."

Vietnamese version:

"VietEd giup hoc sinh lop 9 cai thien tieng Anh nhanh hon bang AI phan tich loi sai va tao bai luyen dung diem yeu."

## Why This Wedge

Grade 9 English is a strong starting point because:

- Demand is large across school support and exam prep
- Parents already pay for English improvement
- English supports frequent, short practice sessions
- AI adds clear value in explanation, correction, and targeted repetition
- Learning progress is easier to measure than many subjective subjects

This wedge is sharper than a broad "AI for all subjects" product and more differentiated than a generic quiz app.

## Target Users

### 1. Primary user: Grade 9 students

- Need help with school English and regular test prep
- Often know they are weak, but do not know exactly why
- Need short, guided sessions instead of long theory-heavy lessons
- Respond well to immediate correction and visible progress

### 2. Secondary user: Parents

- Want confidence that study time leads to measurable improvement
- Need simple summaries of weak skills and next actions
- Prefer clear outcomes over lots of content

## Problem

Most English learning options for Vietnamese students fall into one of these buckets:

- Broad learning platforms with too many features and weak personalization
- Test banks that show scores but do not repair mistakes
- Generic AI chat that explains, but does not track skill gaps over time

Students do more exercises, but the same mistakes keep repeating. Parents see activity, but not real diagnosis or focused next steps.

## Product Opportunity

VietEd should not try to win on content quantity first.

VietEd should win by becoming the fastest way for a Grade 9 student to:

- know what they are weak at
- understand why they got it wrong
- retry targeted practice immediately
- return to the same weak skills until they become stable

## Core User Story

"I choose a Grade 9 English topic, get a short explanation in Vietnamese, take a mini quiz, see exactly which skills I got wrong, and receive the right follow-up practice to improve faster."

## Product Principles

- Vietnamese-first explanations
- Skill-first diagnosis, not chapter-only scores
- Short sessions by default
- Hint before answer
- Review based on real mistakes, not random repetition
- Progress should feel concrete to both students and parents

## Core Learning Loop

### 1. Diagnose

The student takes a short topic-based quiz or a broader diagnostic check.

### 2. Explain

VietEd explains mistakes in simple Vietnamese with small examples and just enough theory.

### 3. Repair

The system generates a targeted retry set focused only on the weak skill.

### 4. Review

Weak skills return in future daily review sessions using spaced repetition and confidence signals.

### 5. Track

The student and parent can see improvement by skill over time.

## Scope For MVP

### Subject

- English

### Grade band

- Grade 9

### Main use case

- Daily school support plus exam preparation

### Core promise

- Faster improvement through AI-guided mistake repair

## Skill Areas For The First Version

The MVP should track performance by skill category, not just by lesson or chapter.

Recommended starting skills:

- Grammar tenses
- Sentence structure
- Word forms
- Vocabulary meaning
- Synonyms and antonyms
- Prepositions and collocations
- Reading comprehension
- Error identification
- Short sentence transformation

## MVP Features

### 1. Student profile

- Name
- Grade
- Daily study goal
- Preferred explanation depth

### 2. Grade 9 English curriculum map

- Units and topics aligned to common Grade 9 learning goals
- Topic to skill mapping

### 3. Diagnostic quiz

- Quick initial assessment
- Topic-level and skill-level weakness detection

### 4. AI explanation layer

- Explains why an answer is wrong in Vietnamese
- Gives one short example
- Offers a hint-first teaching style
- Supports explanation depth such as short, easy, or detailed

### 5. Targeted retry sets

- Generate follow-up practice focused on mistakes from the last quiz
- Move from easier to harder items within the same skill

### 6. Daily review queue

- Bring back weak skills automatically
- Use correctness plus confidence to prioritize review

### 7. Progress dashboard

- Accuracy by skill
- Mastery trend
- Study streak
- Recently improved skills
- Skills needing attention

### 8. Parent summary

- What improved this week
- What is still weak
- What the student should study next

## Non-MVP Features

Save for later:

- Full speaking evaluation
- Teacher dashboards
- Large content marketplace
- Multi-grade expansion
- All-subject support
- Live tutoring

## AI Jobs To Be Done

The AI should:

- Explain a Grade 9 English concept in Vietnamese
- Identify the likely reason an answer is wrong
- Tag the mistake to a skill
- Create a short targeted retry set
- Decide what should be reviewed next
- Adjust explanation depth to the student

The AI should not:

- Drift into generic open chat without topic grounding
- Give final answers too quickly when the student is struggling
- Invent curriculum structure on the fly

## Success Metrics

### Student metrics

- Quiz completion rate
- Retry-set completion rate
- Review return rate
- Weekly active learners
- Improvement in weak-skill accuracy over time

### Parent and product metrics

- Parent summary open rate
- Day 7 retention
- Sessions per week
- Time to first visible improvement

## Competitive Angle

Large platforms such as OLM win on breadth, school integration, and ecosystem scale.

VietEd should win on:

- sharper student focus
- better mistake intelligence
- faster feedback loops
- simpler parent-facing clarity
- a stronger feeling of "this AI knows exactly where I am weak"

Positioning comparison:

- OLM helps students learn broadly across a large platform
- VietEd helps Grade 9 students improve English faster by repairing the exact mistakes lowering their performance

## Sharp Recommendation

Do not launch as a general English app for everyone.

Launch as a focused product for Grade 9 English with one clear promise:

"Understand mistakes faster. Repair weak skills faster. Improve faster."
